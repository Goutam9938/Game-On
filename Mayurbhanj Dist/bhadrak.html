LOCAL CULTURES & FOOD

Festivals: Makar Sankranti, Maha Shivaratri, Durga Puja.
Handicrafts: Mayurbhanj Bamboo Crafts, Handloom Textiles.
Art Forms: Santhal Dance, Paika Dance.
Heritage: Kichakeswari Temple, Simlipal National Park.
Mahaprasad: The sacred offerings at Kichakeswari Temple.
Chhena Poda: A traditional sweet made from chhena (Indian cottage cheese), popular in Mayurbhanj.
Dalma: A vegetarian dish made with lentils and vegetables, a staple in Odisha.
