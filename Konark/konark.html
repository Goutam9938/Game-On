Konark Sun Temple

A Marvel of Ancient Architecture

About Konark Sun Temple

The Konark Sun Temple, also known as the Black Pagoda, is a 13th-century temple dedicated to the Sun God, Surya. Located in Konark, Odisha, it is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is known for its magnificent chariot-shaped architecture, intricate carvings, and stunning sculptures. The temple's architecture represents the Sun God's chariot, with twelve pairs of wheels and seven horses, showcasing the brilliance of ancient Indian craftsmanship. The temple is an outstanding example of Kalinga architecture and is an important pilgrimage site for Hindus.

Famous Food & Culture

Konark is known for its rich cultural heritage and traditional Odiya cuisine. Some of the popular dishes include:

  • Dalma: A lentil and vegetable stew, often served with rice.
  • Chhena Poda: A sweet dish made of cottage cheese, baked with sugar and cardamom.
  • Kanika: A sweet pulao made with rice, jaggery, and ghee.

Nearby Tourist Attractions

  • Puri Jagannath Temple: A sacred temple of Lord Jagannath located in Puri, about 35 km from Konark.
  • Chilika Lake: A brackish water lagoon, home to a variety of migratory birds, about 50 km from Konark.
  • Raghurajpur Heritage Village: A traditional artisan village famous for its Pattachitra paintings and craftwork, located about 14 km from Konark.

How to Get There

Mode Details
By Air The nearest airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport, Bhubaneswar (about 65 km away).
By Train The nearest Railway Station is at Konark, which is around 3 km distance from the temple.
By Road Konark is well-connected by road, and the temple is easily accessible from the town center.

Plan Your Visit

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Konark Sun Temple is from **October to March**, when the weather is pleasant.

Local Customs

Visitors are expected to dress modestly while visiting the temple. It is customary to offer flowers to the deity as a mark of respect.

Accommodation

Konark offers a range of accommodation options, from budget hotels to luxury resorts, for a comfortable stay.
