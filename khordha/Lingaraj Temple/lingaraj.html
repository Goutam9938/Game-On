Lingaraj Temple

A Divine Marvel in the Heart of Bhubaneswar

About Lingaraj Temple

The Lingaraj Temple, one of the most significant temples in Odisha, is dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located in the heart of Bhubaneswar, this 11th-century architectural masterpiece is an exemplary representation of Kalinga architecture. The temple complex, with its towering spire and intricately sculpted walls, stands as a remarkable symbol of ancient craftsmanship. The Lingaraj Temple is one of the largest temples in the city and is an important pilgrimage destination, attracting devotees from all over the world.

Famous Food & Culture

Bhubaneswar and the Lingaraj Temple are not only rich in spirituality but also in culinary delights. Some of the popular dishes include:

  • Dalma: A traditional lentil stew with vegetables, often served with rice.
  • Chhena Poda: A famous sweet made from cottage cheese, sugar, and cardamom.
  • Rasabali: A dessert made from chhena (cottage cheese) and flavored with cardamom and jaggery.

Nearby Tourist Attractions

  • Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves: Ancient rock-cut caves dating back to the 2nd century BC, located about 10 km from the temple.
  • Nandankanan Zoological Park: A wildlife sanctuary with a variety of species, about 15 km from Lingaraj Temple.
  • Mukteshwar Temple: Another famous Shiva temple known for its architectural beauty, about 6 km from the Lingaraj Temple.

How to Get There

Mode Details
By Air The nearest airport is Biju Patnaik International Airport, Bhubaneswar, about 6 km from the temple.
By Train Bhubaneswar Railway Station is well-connected, with frequent trains to all major cities.
By Road The temple is easily accessible by road from various parts of the city.

Plan Your Visit

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Lingaraj Temple is during the **winter months** (October to March), when the weather is cool and pleasant.

Local Customs

Visitors are expected to dress modestly. It is customary to offer prayers and flowers at the temple for blessings.

Accommodation

Bhubaneswar offers a range of accommodation options, from budget hotels to luxury stays, within close proximity to Lingaraj Temple.
