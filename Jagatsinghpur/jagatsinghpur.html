LOCAL CULTURES & FOOD

Festivals: Rath Yatra, Durga Puja, Makar Sankranti.
Handicrafts: Kantha Work, Pattachitra Paintings.
Art Forms: Odissi Dance, Gotipua Dance.
Heritage: Jagannath Temple, Chilika Lake.
Mahaprasad: Prasad offerings from Jagannath Temple.
Fish Curry: A traditional dish made with fish, a staple of coastal Odisha.
Chingudi Malai Curry: A popular prawn curry made with coconut milk, native to the region.
