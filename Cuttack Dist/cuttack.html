LOCAL CULTURES & FOOD

Festivals: Baliyatra, Durga Puja, Makar Sankranti.
Handicrafts: Silver Filigree work, Brass work, and Patta Chitra.
Art Forms: Odissi dance, Jatra theatre.
Heritage: Barabati Fort, Akhanda Temple.
Mahaprasad: Famous food offerings at the temples.
Chhena Poda: A traditional caramelized cheese dessert.
Rasabali: A sweet dish made from rice and chhena, popular during festivals.
