LOCAL CULTURES & FOOD

Festivals: Raja Festival, Nuakhai, Makar Sankranti.
Handicrafts: Silver Filigree, Handloom Sarees.
Art Forms: Odissi Dance, Pattachitra Painting.
Heritage: Baladevjew Temple, Chandpur Beach.
Mahaprasad: Prasad offerings from Baladevjew Temple.
Fish Curry: A traditional fish curry made with local ingredients.
Chingudi Malai Curry: A prawn curry with coconut milk, very popular in Kendrapara.
